Wli Waterfalls

Located in the Volta Region, Wli is the tallest waterfall in West Africa, surrounded by lush forests and home to thousands of bats. A perfect spot for nature lovers and hikers.

Elmina Castle

Built by the Portuguese in 1482, this is the oldest European building in sub-Saharan Africa. It played a major role in the transatlantic slave trade and remains a significant historical landmark.

Cape Coast Castle

Another key slave trade fortress, this castle offers a moving historical experience with guided tours through dungeons and tunnels once used to hold enslaved Africans before shipment.

Lake Volta

Welcome to GH-Tour

Ghana is a land of rich history, vibrant culture, and breathtaking landscapes. GH-Tour is your ultimate guide to discovering the beauty of Ghana—its stunning tourist attractions, colorful fashion, delicious food, and unique traditions. From the historic Cape Coast Castle to the lively streets of Accra, we take you on a journey through Ghana’s most iconic spots. Whether you're looking for the best markets to shop for Kente and Ankara prints or want to experience the magic of festivals like Chale Wote and Homowo, GH-Tour has got you covered.

Food, Lifestyle & Event

Ghana is a vibrant blend of food, fashion, and festivals, where jollof rice, Kente styles, and lively celebrations define everyday life. From bustling markets to highlife music nights, and festivals like Chale Wote and Homowo, every moment is rich with culture. GH-Tour invites you to experience it all!
